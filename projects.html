Human Motion Prediction (META)

  • Used a subset from the largest Archive of Motion Capture as Surface Shapes (AMASS) to test a new model for motion prediction: Temporal Convolutional Networks (TCN). Used Average Displacement Error (ADE) and hypertuned parameters to optimize the TCN. TCN successfully outperformed baseline models of optimized RNN and Transformer-Encoder using MAE and MSE loss
